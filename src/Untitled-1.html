Etymology[edit]
\n
From Old Khmer សភា (sabhā, “council, court of justice; member of a council or court; council chamber, tribunal”), ultimately from Pali sabhā (“assembly; congregation; convention”) or Sanskrit सभा (sabhā, “assembly; meeting”). Doublet of សុភា (sophiə). Cognate with Thai สภา (sà-paa), สุภา (sù-paa).\n
